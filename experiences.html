Must-try experiences

Embark on unforgettable adventures in Siquijor with must-try experiences that immerse you in the island’s mystical charm and natural beauty. From ancient healing rituals to vibrant butterfly sanctuaries, these unique encounters promise to leave you with lasting memories and a deeper connection to the island’s culture.

Mount Bandilaan National Park

Experience Healing Rituals

Step into a world of mysticism as you visit a traditional healer and witness Siquijor’s time-honored practices, believed to have the power to restore balance and health.

Butterfly Sanctuary

Explore the Butterflies

Wander through the serene Siquijor Butterfly Sanctuary, where you can marvel at colorful butterflies and learn about the island’s efforts in preserving these delicate creatures.
